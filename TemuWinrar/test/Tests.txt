These are test. Just paste the commands 1 by 1 or in bulk and you will receive the shouwn result.

Basic tests:
help
zip test\arch.zip test\testFolder
zip test\arch.zip test\testFolder\testFile1.txt
info test\arch.zip
check test\arch.zip
unzip test\arch.zip test\results
exit
